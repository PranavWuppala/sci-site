Director's Message

Compassionate, evidence-based cancer care for the people of Andhra Pradesh.

It gives me immense pride to welcome you to the State Cancer Institute, Kurnool — the first Comprehensive Cancer Care centre of its kind in Andhra Pradesh. What began as a vision to bring world-class oncology services closer to the people of our state has today grown into a fully operational, 120-bedded tertiary cancer hospital offering the complete spectrum of cancer care under one roof.

Our institute is built on the conviction that geography should never determine a patient's access to quality treatment. Every day, our multi-disciplinary team of oncologists, surgeons, pathologists, radiologists, anaesthesiologists and palliative care specialists come together to ensure that each patient receives a treatment plan shaped by collective expertise — not a single opinion. This team-based approach to diagnosis and care is at the heart of everything we do.

We are equipped with modern infrastructure that matches the best in the country — from the Elekta Versa HD Linear Accelerator and HDR Brachytherapy unit to a dedicated CT Simulator, modular operation theatres and a fully equipped onco-pathology laboratory. These are not just machines; they are instruments of hope, enabling us to deliver precise, contemporary radiation, surgical and medical oncology care to thousands of patients every year.

Equally close to my heart is our Cancer Pain and Palliative Care unit — the first of its kind in Andhra Pradesh to receive international accreditation. We believe that cancer care does not end with treatment; it extends to the management of pain, the preservation of dignity and the support of families through every stage of the journey.

As a teaching hospital affiliated with Kurnool Medical College, we are also committed to training the next generation of oncologists — young doctors who will carry forward the mission of compassionate, evidence-based cancer care to every corner of our state.

I invite you to explore our website to learn more about our services, our team and our ongoing research. If you or a loved one needs care, know that we are here — and that every resource of this institute is devoted to your well-being.

Dr. C.S.K. Prakash
Founding Director, State Cancer Institute, Kurnool

About Us

An institution serving Andhra Pradesh

The State Cancer Institute, Kurnool functions under the Department of Health, Medical & Family Welfare, Government of Andhra Pradesh. It serves as a tertiary referral centre for surgical, medical and radiation oncology for the people of Andhra Pradesh.

Affiliated with Kurnool Medical College for academic training and empanelled under Dr. YSR Aarogyasri & AB-PMJAY for cashless treatment.
