CSR & Donors

Corporate, philanthropic and community partnerships that strengthen cancer care at the Institute.

Why Partner With Us

Every contribution reaches a patient

The State Cancer Institute, Kurnool serves a large catchment of patients — the majority of whom belong to economically weaker sections. While Aarogyasri and AB-PMJAY cover most clinical treatment, gaps remain in transport, nutrition, accommodation for attendants, palliative care, and supportive infrastructure.

CSR funds and donations have directly helped us deliver clean drinking water, transport patients on time for radiation sessions, strengthen day-care chemotherapy, and expand palliative support.

We acknowledge every partner — corporate, trust, NGO and individual — who has chosen to stand with our patients.

Impact So Far

What CSR partnerships have enabled

RO Water Plant

  • Clean drinking water for patients and attendants across the campus.
  • Reduces water-borne infection risk in immunocompromised patients.
  • Funded under corporate CSR partnership.

Water Cooler Systems

  • Installed across OPD, wards and day-care chemotherapy.
  • Free chilled water access through summer months.
  • Supplemented by NGO and donor contributions.

EV for Patient Transport

  • Electric vehicle for free transport of patients to and from radiation sessions.
  • Critical for patients from rural districts on multi-week radiation schedules.
  • Reduces drop-outs from prescribed treatment.

Day-Care Chemotherapy

  • Strengthened infusion chairs, monitoring and supportive medication stock.
  • Enables more outpatient chemotherapy — patients return home the same day.
  • Reduced waiting time for chemotherapy slots.

Palliative Care

  • Enhanced palliative medicine stock, dressings and home-care kits.
  • Supports the ESMO-designated integrated oncology & palliative care programme.
  • Patient and family counselling services.

Awareness & Screening

  • Funded community cancer awareness camps in nearby districts.
  • Tobacco cessation and HPV awareness drives.
  • Supports early detection — better outcomes, lower cost of care.

Ways to Contribute

How you can help

Corporate CSR

  • Equipment grants — infusion pumps, monitors, diagnostic kits.
  • Infrastructure — water, power backup, patient amenities.
  • Sponsor a treatment cycle, awareness camp or screening drive.

Trusts & NGOs

  • Nutrition support for inpatients and attendants.
  • Accommodation for patients from distant districts.
  • Palliative and survivorship programmes.

Individual Donations

  • One-time or recurring contributions through institute accounts.
  • Sponsor a chemo cycle for a needy patient.
  • In-kind donations — wheelchairs, blankets, patient kits.
