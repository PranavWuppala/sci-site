Diagnostics

Onco-Pathology & diagnostic services

The Onco-Pathology department supports the institute's clinical decision-making through routine histopathology, cytology and advanced immunohistochemistry. The institute also participates in the Hospital Based Cancer Registry (HBCR) funded by ICMR, contributing regional cancer epidemiology data at the national level.

🔬

Onco-Pathology

  • Histopathology & surgical specimens
  • Frozen section reporting (linked to OT)
  • Immunohistochemistry (IHC)
  • Fine Needle Aspiration Cytology (FNAC)
  • Fluid Cytology
  • Bone Marrow Aspiration & Biopsy
📊

  • Immunohistochemistry (IHC) — 267
  • FNAC — 165

Preventive Oncology

Preventive Oncology & screening

A dedicated Preventive Oncology OP runs at the institute, focused on early detection, awareness and community outreach. Screening is conducted regularly for the most common preventable and detectable cancers in the region, with community-based mobile screening services in planning.

🎗️

Cancer Screening

  • Breast cancer screening (clinical & mammography)
  • Cervical cancer screening (Pap smear / VIA)
  • Oral cancer screening
  • Survivorship clinic
📣

Awareness & Outreach

  • Community awareness camps
  • Mobile screening service (in planning)
  • School & college outreach for HPV awareness
  • Patient education material in Telugu

Supportive Care

Supportive services

The institute is an ESMO-designated centre of integrated oncology and palliative care — one of only 23 such centres in India. Plans are under way to expand the current 8-bed unit into a dedicated 50-bed palliative care facility.

🤝

Palliative & Pain

  • ESMO-designated centre (1 of 23 in India)
  • Dedicated 8-bed Cancer Pain & Palliative Care Unit
  • End-of-life & hospice care
  • Home-based palliative outreach
  • Bereavement counselling
🚐

Patient Logistics

  • Electric vehicle for patient transportation (CSR)
  • RO water plant & cooler systems
  • Day-care chemotherapy strengthening
  • Patient-attendant amenities
