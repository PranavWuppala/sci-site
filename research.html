Research & Academics

Advancing cancer knowledge through clinical research, academic training and continuing medical education tailored to the regional disease profile.

Current Focus

Ongoing research

Epidemiology & Outcomes

  • Epidemiology of head & neck cancers in Rayalaseema region.
  • Outcomes of concurrent chemo-radiation in carcinoma cervix.
  • Survival outcomes for stage III breast cancer (regional cohort).
  • Treatment delay patterns and their effect on outcomes.

Prevention & Screening

  • HPV vaccination uptake — district-level intervention study.
  • Tobacco cessation outcomes in OPD patients.
  • Oral cancer screening yield in dental OPDs.
  • Community awareness on early symptoms (breast, oral, cervical).

Technology & AI

  • AI-assisted cancer screening pilot (in collaboration).
  • Voice-based clinical scribing in oncology OPD — workflow study.
  • Tele-oncology referral pathways for district hospitals.
  • Digital pathology — workflow validation.

Training

Academic programmes

Super-specialty

  • MCh — Surgical Oncology
  • DM — Medical Oncology
  • MD — Radiation Oncology

Allied courses

  • Diploma in Onco-Nursing
  • Diploma in Medical Radiation Technology
  • Fellowship programmes in palliative medicine

Continuing Education

  • Quarterly CMEs across oncology subspecialties
  • State-wide tumour board sessions
  • Workshops for district-level clinicians
  • Patient education material in Telugu

Output

Publications & presentations

Faculty publications appear in peer-reviewed Indian and international journals including the Indian Journal of Cancer, IJMR and others. Annual presentations are made at state and national oncology conferences.

The Institute also publishes treatment protocols and patient-facing education material in Telugu, used across the regional primary and secondary care network for referral and counselling.
